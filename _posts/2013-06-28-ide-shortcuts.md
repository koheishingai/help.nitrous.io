**Save file**

    'Command + S'

**Refresh directory tree**

    'Control + Shift + R'

**New tab**

    'Control + Shift + N'

**Close tab**

    'Control + Shift + W'

**Select next tab**

    'Control + Shift + ]'

**Select previous tab**

    'Control + Shift + ['

**Focus console**

    'Control + Shift + C'

**Toggle full-screen console**

    'Control + Shift + F'

#### Copy/Paste (Windows Users)

**Copy**: `Shift + Ctrl + c`

**Paste**:`Shift + Ctrl + v`

#### Copy/Paste (Mac Users)

**Copy**: `Command + c`

**Paste**:`Command + v`
