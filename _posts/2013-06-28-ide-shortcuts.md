**New tab**

    'Control + Shift + N'

**Save file**

    'Command + S'

**Refresh directory tree**

    'Control + Shift + R'

**Close tab**

    'Control + Shift + W'

**Select next tab**

    'Control + Shift + ]'

**Select previous tab**

    'Control + Shift + ['

**Focus console**

    'Control + Shift + C'

**Toggle full-screen console**

    'Control + Shift + F'
